About Management

Department of management is constituted with the philosophy of managing the programs with the focus on academic excellence through innovation. Department of management facilitates students of graduation level of various engineering fields to attain knowledge about solving problems of organization, planning abilities of management for complex engineering driven enterprises. The faculty is highly qualified and motivated to inculcate quest for perfection among students.

Emphasis is laid on the following areas :

  • Making a blend of management and technology so as a student can manage engineering & technology oriented business.
  • Grooming the personalities of the students
  • Developing managerial skills among students for decision making
