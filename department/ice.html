INSTRUMENTATION & CONTROL ENGINEERING

About Instrumentation & Control Engineering

Instrumentation and Control Engineering is a multidisciplinary branch which includes electrical, electronics and instrumentation. It enriches in the area of automation where the students are exposed to various subjects related to measurements and control. The department takes an intake of 60 students each year. The Department has got highly qualified and experienced faculty members. They have expertise in the area of the control system, power electronics etc. Department consists of well-equipped Labs, highly qualified and skilled Instructors.

Vision :

Excellence in instrumentation and control engineering education through innovative practices, teamwork, and research to develop cutting-edge automation solutions. We work to create a better future every day.

Mission :

  • To inculcate research and innovative qualities in multidisciplinary engineering domain with continuous knowledge.
  • To upgrade skill in order to enhance sustainable development of global community.
  • To nurture the passion for learning and an attitude to serve the society.

Scope :

Nature of work of an instrumentation engineer ranges from designing, developing, installing, managing equipment that are used to monitor and control machinery. Instruments at home and elsewhere is only because of the science of instrumentation.
